About Us

We're dedicated to empowering small businesses

Our Story

ShopTrack was born from the challenges faced by local shopkeepers in managing their daily operations. We saw the need for a simple yet powerful solution that doesn't require technical expertise.

Our team of retail experts and developers worked closely with shop owners to create a platform that addresses real-world problems and makes running a business easier.

Today, we're proud to help thousands of shopkeepers across the country streamline their operations and focus on what matters most - serving their customers.
